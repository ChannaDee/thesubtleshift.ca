Oracle Card Readings

  • Channeled Messages: Direct communication from your guides and angels

What to Expect

You will receive clear insights about your soul's journey, current life circumstances, and guidance for your highest good. The session includes both verbal guidance and energetic transmissions to support your spiritual growth.

Sessions range from 30 to 60 minutes depending on the depth of guidance that comes through for you.
